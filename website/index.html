NutriGenie is an app for tracking your intake of nutrients. Start by setting your user preferences: height, weight, age and gender and have the app calculate your recommended daily calories. Next, press scan to open up the camera and photograph the nutrition label of a food you ate today. The app then displays a bar chart that shows the percent of your daily amount for each nutrient. You can choose to keep or discard the scan. If you keep the scan the information is uploaded to your online profile. Now on the home screen you can see your daily totals for each nutrient (expressed as percentages). Finally, you can view a history of the amount of each nutrient you ate for the past week displayed as a line chart.
